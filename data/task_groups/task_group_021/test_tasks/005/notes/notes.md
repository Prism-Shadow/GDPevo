# Test 005 — April Freight Close and Containment

## English

### Problem and transfer basis

This test transfers the transaction/reference controls from train 002 and train 005 to an April freight close. It adds integrated invoice evidence-release reconstruction, unsafe-union graph containment, and certified-versus-provisional FX counterfactual exposure.

### Solution basis

Reconstruct source scope and duplicate charges, resolve alias policy, adjudicate every retained charge, and keep source, recognition, conversion, accrual, and ledger controls consistent. Produce exact mismatch and quarantine populations, normalized and lane results, carrier ranking, integrated close evidence and routing, unsafe graph containment certificates, and FX counterfactuals.

### Materials and pitfalls

The scope payload defines controlled codes, invoice routes, graph scenarios, containment stages, and FX comparisons. The environment supplies freight charges, references, units, FX, snapshots, and query access. Pitfalls include aggregating before deduplication, mixing source and ledger controls, using provisional FX in certified totals, omitting non-focus invoices, or treating the containment stages independently instead of as one sequential plan.

### Exact evaluation

Ten disjoint exact-result bundles use weights `3,2,3,2,3,1,3,3,3,2`. They cover source/duplicates, alias policy, charge adjudication, ledger controls, exception populations, normalized lane results, carrier ranking, integrated close evidence and routing, containment certificates, and FX exposure. Every declared path in a point must match exactly.

## 中文

### 问题与迁移依据

本测试把 train 002 和 train 005 的交易与参考控制迁移到四月份货运结账，并增加整合发票证据释放重建、不安全并集图隔离，以及认证汇率与临时汇率的反事实暴露。

### 解题依据

重建来源范围和重复费用，解析别名政策，裁决每笔保留费用，并保持来源、识别、换算、应计和台账控制一致。输出精确的不匹配与隔离群体、归一化与线路结果、承运商排序、整合结账证据和路由、不安全图隔离证书及汇率反事实。

### 材料与易错点

范围载荷定义受控代码、发票路由、图场景、隔离阶段和汇率比较；环境提供货运费用、参考、单位、汇率、快照和查询接口。易错点包括在去重前汇总、混淆来源与台账控制、在认证总额中使用临时汇率、遗漏非焦点发票，或把连续隔离阶段当作互不相关的结果。

### 精确评测

十个互不重叠的精确结果包权重为 `3,2,3,2,3,1,3,3,3,2`，覆盖来源/重复、别名政策、费用裁决、台账控制、异常群体、归一化线路结果、承运商排序、整合结账证据与路由、隔离证书和汇率暴露。评分点中的每条声明路径都必须精确匹配。
