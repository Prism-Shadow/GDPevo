# Test 004 — Warranty Outreach Identity Safety

## English

### Problem and transfer basis

This test applies the two contact train tasks to warranty outreach, then extends them with ordered snapshot replay, unsafe-identifier and channel-transfer adjudication, graph impact analysis, household remediation, and source-outage restoration.

### Solution basis

Reconstruct the baseline scope, canonical owners, memberships, field values, policy state, and readiness. Adjudicate identifier cases and the complete derived transfer population without unsafe cross-owner channel copying. Replay source admission in order, evaluate unsafe graph impacts, optimize the household remediation portfolio, and solve the source-removal restoration portfolio.

### Materials and pitfalls

The scope payload provides controlled codes, replay order, unsafe-link definitions, budgets, and tie breaks. The shared environment provides contact rows and source metadata. Pitfalls include name-only merges, transferring contact data across unsafe identifiers, confusing canonical field precedence with identity membership, omitting any derived transfer candidate, or optimizing against a non-baseline owner population.

### Exact evaluation

Ten disjoint exact-result bundles use weights `3,1,3,3,1,1,2,1,1,1`. The quarantined-row count is the single low-weight diagnostic point; most weight remains on population reconstruction, owner linkage, canonical fields, and provenance. Identifier and transfer safety form one exact control outcome, and graph impact plus its household remediation form one exact safety analysis. Any wrong field fails its owning point.

## 中文

### 问题与迁移依据

本测试把两个联系人训练题的方法应用到质保触达，并扩展到有序快照回放、不安全标识符与渠道转移裁决、图影响分析、家庭修复和来源中断恢复。

### 解题依据

重建基线范围、规范车主、成员关系、字段值、政策状态和就绪度；裁决标识符案例及完整派生转移群体，禁止通过不安全跨车主标识复制渠道；按顺序回放来源加入，评估不安全图影响，优化家庭修复组合，并求解来源移除后的恢复组合。

### 材料与易错点

范围载荷提供受控代码、回放顺序、不安全连接定义、预算和并列规则；共享环境提供联系人行和来源元数据。易错点包括按姓名合并、通过不安全标识符跨车主转移联系方式、混淆字段优先级与身份成员关系、遗漏派生候选，或对非基线群体做优化。

### 精确评测

十个互不重叠的精确结果包权重为 `3,1,3,3,1,1,2,1,1,1`。隔离行数是唯一低权重诊断点；主要权重落在群体重建、车主关联、规范字段和来源优先级。标识符与渠道转移安全构成一个精确控制结果，图影响及其家庭修复构成一个精确安全分析。任一错误字段都会击穿所属评分点。
