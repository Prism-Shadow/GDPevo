## English

Data and lineage: This task belongs to `SCN_019_regulatory_licensing_eligibility_and_compliance_review`, derived from source examples `E001`, `E002`, and `E003`, with direct workflow alignment to `E001` Washington contractor eligibility review. The shared generated environment is `task_group/task_group_019/env/`, backed by `env/data/licensing.db` and generated by `env/generate_data.py` with seed `19019`. The target ids are the manifest group `train_004`: `C-TR4-001` through `C-TR4-007`.

Task definition: The solver is asked to act as a senior contractor licensing examiner and produce a structured eligibility review. Solver-visible input is limited to `input/prompt.txt`, `input/payloads/answer_template.json`, and the public business endpoints rooted at `<TASK_ENV_BASE_URL>`. The answer must classify each target application as `APPROVE`, `HOLD`, or `DENY`; provide controlled deficiency and required-action codes; assign a risk tier; flag current-policy impact; and complete summary rollups.

Scenario fit: The task exercises the same administrative licensing pattern as the source contractor example: current policy standards must be reconciled with application records, bond and insurance evidence, prior license status, complaint and violation history, correspondence, and inspection notes. It is a formal train task, not a tutorial. The standard answer exposes a solved example from which later skill generation can infer source-precedence habits and decision boundaries.

Material map: `/api/policies` provides the current contractor thresholds and the legacy baseline. `/api/contractor/applications` provides trade, requested class, endorsement status, prior license id, and experience. `/api/contractor/bonds` and `/api/contractor/insurance` provide current financial-security evidence. `/api/contractor/license-history` identifies the active suspension for `C-TR4-007`. `/api/contractor/violations` identifies the open serious conflict for `C-TR4-003` and resolved or dismissed distractors for other files. `/api/contractor/correspondence` provides unverified or conflicting correspondence ids for the summary. `/api/contractor/inspections` supplies context but is not the primary eligibility blocker in this task.

Solution basis: Use 2025-07-18 as the review date. Current policy minimums by trade/class are taken from the contractor policy rows. Active bond and active, unexpired insurance are required. Missing or pending endorsement, experience below the policy minimum, bond shortfall, cancelled bond, and non-current insurance are hold-level correctable deficiencies unless paired with an enforcement blocker. An open serious complaint or violation blocks release and is treated as denial-level. An active prior-license suspension is also denial-level. Current-policy impact is true when the current 2025 policy creates a material deficiency or review flag that would not have applied under the prior baseline, especially endorsement or higher bond requirements.

Standard answer summary: `C-TR4-004` is approved. `C-TR4-001`, `C-TR4-002`, `C-TR4-005`, and `C-TR4-006` are held for correctable documentation, financial-security, endorsement, or experience issues. `C-TR4-003` is denied because the open serious unpermitted-work conflict blocks release in addition to other deficiencies. `C-TR4-007` is denied because the prior license is actively suspended, with additional experience and insurance deficiencies. High-risk ids are `C-TR4-003` and `C-TR4-007`. Policy-impacted ids are `C-TR4-002`, `C-TR4-003`, `C-TR4-005`, and `C-TR4-006`. Stale or unverified correspondence ids are `COR-C-TR4-002-1`, `COR-C-TR4-005-1`, `COR-DIS-0114`, and `COR-DIS-0117`.

Evaluation basis: The evaluator has eight whole scoring points with raw weights 3, 3, 3, 3, 2, 2, 1, and 1. They score the complete determination set; financial-security deficiencies and actions; endorsement and experience deficiencies and actions; enforcement blockers; risk tiers and high-risk summary; policy impact flags and summary; determination counts; and stale or unverified correspondence ids. Each point is pass/fail with no partial credit inside the point, and every scored string-like result is a controlled enum or code from `answer_template.json`.

Transfer design: As a train task, `train_004` anchors contractor-specific transfer for later contractor test tasks. A skill can infer that current policy rows govern thresholds, active/current financial records matter more than old records or unverified correspondence, correctable documentation problems usually hold an application, resolved or dismissed violations are not automatic denials, and active suspension or unresolved serious enforcement conflicts are high-risk release blockers. It also reinforces the output conventions for application decisions, high-risk summaries, policy-impact summaries, and controlled deficiency/action codes.

Likely model pitfalls: Common mistakes include treating all inspection findings as automatic holds, relying on unverified correspondence to cure a registry problem, overlooking insurance expiration despite an `active` status, treating resolved/dismissed violations as denial-level, approving files with pending endorsement, or forgetting to compare current policy impact to the legacy baseline.

## 中文

数据与来源：本任务属于 `SCN_019_regulatory_licensing_eligibility_and_compliance_review`，来源示例为 `E001`、`E002`、`E003`，其中工作流最直接对应 `E001` 的承包商资格审查。共享生成环境位于 `task_group/task_group_019/env/`，数据由 `env/generate_data.py` 以种子 `19019` 生成，并存储在 `env/data/licensing.db`。目标申请来自 manifest 中的 `train_004` 分组：`C-TR4-001` 到 `C-TR4-007`。

任务定义：求解者扮演高级承包商许可审查员，输出结构化资格审查结果。可见输入仅包括 `input/prompt.txt`、`input/payloads/answer_template.json`，以及以 `<TASK_ENV_BASE_URL>` 为根的业务接口。答案需要为每个目标申请给出 `APPROVE`、`HOLD` 或 `DENY`，填写受控缺陷代码和补救动作代码，给出风险等级，标记现行政策影响，并完成批次摘要。

场景匹配：本任务复用了源承包商示例中的行政许可模式：需要把现行政策、申请记录、bond、insurance、既往许可证状态、投诉和违规记录、往来函件、现场记录放在一起判断。它是正式训练任务，不是教程。标准答案可以让后续技能生成推断来源优先级和审查边界。

材料地图：`/api/policies` 提供现行承包商门槛和旧基线。`/api/contractor/applications` 提供工种、申请类别、endorsement 状态、既往许可证号和经验年限。`/api/contractor/bonds` 与 `/api/contractor/insurance` 提供财务保障证据。`/api/contractor/license-history` 标识 `C-TR4-007` 的有效暂停状态。`/api/contractor/violations` 标识 `C-TR4-003` 的未结严重问题，并提供其他已解决或驳回的干扰记录。`/api/contractor/correspondence` 用于摘要中的未核实或冲突函件。`/api/contractor/inspections` 提供背景信息，但不是本任务的主要资格阻断来源。

解答依据：审查日期为 2025-07-18。各工种和类别的现行门槛来自 contractor policy 记录。需要有效 bond 和有效且未过期的 insurance。endorsement 缺失或待定、经验不足、bond 不足或取消、insurance 非当前有效，通常是可补正的 HOLD 缺陷；若同时存在强制执行阻断，则进入更高等级处理。未结严重投诉或违规阻断放行，按 DENY 处理。既往许可证处于 active suspension 也按 DENY 处理。当前政策影响为 true，表示 2025 年现行政策相对旧基线新增了实质缺陷或审查标记，尤其是 endorsement 或提高后的 bond 要求。

标准答案摘要：`C-TR4-004` 可批准。`C-TR4-001`、`C-TR4-002`、`C-TR4-005`、`C-TR4-006` 因可补正的文档、财务保障、endorsement 或经验问题被 HOLD。`C-TR4-003` 因未结严重无证施工冲突而 DENY，同时还有其他缺陷。`C-TR4-007` 因既往许可证 active suspension 而 DENY，同时还有经验和 insurance 缺陷。高风险申请为 `C-TR4-003` 和 `C-TR4-007`。政策影响申请为 `C-TR4-002`、`C-TR4-003`、`C-TR4-005`、`C-TR4-006`。未核实或冲突函件为 `COR-C-TR4-002-1`、`COR-C-TR4-005-1`、`COR-DIS-0114`、`COR-DIS-0117`。

评测依据：评测器包含八个整点评分项，原始权重为 3、3、3、3、2、2、1、1。分别检查完整 determination 集合、财务保障缺陷和动作、endorsement 与经验缺陷和动作、强制执行阻断、风险等级和高风险摘要、政策影响标记和摘要、determination 计数、未核实或冲突函件集合。每个评分项都是通过或不通过，项内没有部分分；所有被评分的字符串结果都来自 `answer_template.json` 中的受控枚举或代码。

迁移设计：作为训练任务，`train_004` 为后续 contractor 测试任务提供迁移锚点。技能生成可以从中推断：现行 policy 记录决定门槛；当前有效的财务记录优先于旧记录或未核实函件；可补正文档问题通常导致 HOLD；已解决或驳回的违规不是自动 DENY；active suspension 和未结严重强制执行问题是高风险放行阻断。本任务也强化了 application decision、high-risk summary、policy-impact summary 以及受控缺陷和动作代码的输出约定。

常见错误：容易把所有 inspection findings 都当成自动 HOLD；用未核实 correspondence 修复 registry 问题；忽略 `active` insurance 已经过期；把已解决或驳回的违规当成 DENY；批准 endorsement pending 的申请；或者忘记把当前政策影响与旧基线比较。
