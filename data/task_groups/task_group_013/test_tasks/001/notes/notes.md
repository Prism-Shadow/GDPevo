# test_001 Notes - New Patient Access Verification: July Community Intake

## English

Data/source lineage: This task belongs to `SCN_013_healthcare_patient_intake_transfer` and uses the patient access validation operation family. It is the July test counterpart to `train_001`, with secondary transfer support from `train_004` for chart/contact and risk-readiness distinctions. The shared generated environment is `task_group/task_group_013/env/`, backed by `env/data/clinic.db`. The task-local payloads are `input/payloads/target_roster.json` and `input/payloads/answer_template.json`.

Task definition: The solver receives roster `NPI-JUL-02` and patient IDs `P007` through `P013`. The requested service date is `2026-07-09`, and the service line is `primary_care`, both recorded in `intake_rosters`. The required answer is a JSON access-verification result with insurance status, prescription benefit status, preferred-pharmacy network status, lifestyle risk, overall risk, registration status, blocked reason codes, and cohort summary counts.

Scenario fit and material map: This is a realistic intake-office access verification task. `intake_rosters` identifies the target cohort and date. `patients` supplies demographic and contact completeness. `coverage` supplies primary payer, policy, effective and termination dates, network state, service-line coverage, and status. `pbm` supplies prescription benefit state and policy matching. `patient_pharmacy` and `pharmacies` determine the rank-1 preferred pharmacy network result. `lifestyle` supplies smoking, alcohol, and exercise values. `clinical_history` supplies chronic-condition burden, medication/allergy complexity, hospitalizations, and risk flags. `chart_artifacts` is available through the portal; in this cohort only `P010` has a current vitals artifact, which does not clear the high-risk access review.

Solution basis: Coverage is valid only when active, date-valid on `2026-07-09`, in network, and covering `primary_care`. PBM is valid only when active, approved, and matching the coverage payer-policy pair. Pharmacy status uses the patient's rank-1 preferred pharmacy only. Lifestyle scoring follows the train anchor: smoking Never/Former/Current = 0/1/2, alcohol None/Occasional/Moderate/Heavy = 0/1/2/3, and exercise 5+ = -1, 3-4 = 0, 1-2 = 1, None or missing = 2; scores <= 1 are low, 2-3 medium, and >= 4 high. Overall risk is high for high lifestyle risk, recent acute utilization, explicit complex medication reconciliation, or the combination of heavy chronic burden and medication/allergy complexity; otherwise moderate clinical complexity produces medium risk. Rejection takes precedence for no usable primary-care coverage, including expired coverage, excluded service line, or out-of-network primary coverage; otherwise high overall risk yields `clinical_review`; administrative-only blockers yield `hold`; all gates clear yields `approved`.

Key answer evidence: `P007` has active primary-care coverage but the coverage is out of network, plus high overall risk from chronic burden and medication complexity. `P008` has expired Cigna coverage and an out-of-network rank-1 pharmacy, while PBM remains policy-matched and approved. `P009` has valid coverage and in-network pharmacy but inactive rejected PBM; risk is medium, so the final status is an administrative hold. `P010` has valid coverage but pending PBM and high risk from lifestyle plus acute-utilization/medication-reconciliation flags, so it requires clinical review. `P011` has coverage ending `2026-06-30`, an out-of-network preferred pharmacy, missing SMS phone contact, and high risk from chronic burden and medications. `P012` has pending coverage that does not include `primary_care`; PBM and pharmacy pass, but high lifestyle risk remains. `P013` lacks primary-care service-line coverage, has a PBM policy mismatch, an out-of-network preferred pharmacy, missing address, and high risk with recent hospitalization.

Evaluation basis: The evaluator has eight whole scoring points: SP001 insurance statuses, weight 2; SP002 prescription statuses, weight 2; SP003 pharmacy statuses, weight 2; SP004 lifestyle risks, weight 2; SP005 overall risks, weight 2; SP006 registration statuses, weight 3; SP007 blocked reason-code sets, weight 3; SP008 cohort summary counts by registration status and risk, weight 1. The rubric spans insurance validation, prescription benefit validation, pharmacy network validation, lifestyle risk, overall clinical risk, final registration decisions, blocked-reason attribution, and aggregation. Lists are normalized by patient ID, and blocked reason codes are compared as sets. Each point earns all of its assigned weight-normalized score or zero.

Transfer design: The main train anchor is `train_001`, which teaches the same access-verification family: separate coverage, PBM, preferred pharmacy, lifestyle risk, overall risk, contact/demographic blockers, final-status precedence, and cohort summary conventions. `train_004` anchors the habit of treating chart/contact readiness and risk signals as separate from mere record existence. Transfer-dependent scoring goals here are SP001 through SP007, especially recognizing PBM policy mismatch versus invalid PBM, using only the rank-1 preferred pharmacy, carrying high risk into clinical review unless rejection already takes precedence, and listing only failed gates as reason codes. Task-specific difficulty comes from the July service date, new target patients, out-of-network primary coverage, coverage ending before July, a rank-2 in-network pharmacy distractor, and mixed PBM/coverage conflicts.

Likely model pitfalls: Common errors include treating active but out-of-network coverage as valid, missing that `P011` terminates before the July service date, accepting the rank-2 in-network pharmacy for `P008`, treating PBM approval as valid despite `P013` policy mismatch, forgetting that missing exercise increases lifestyle risk, making high-risk patients `hold` instead of `clinical_review` when coverage is otherwise usable, and adding reason codes for gates that passed.

## 中文

数据来源：本任务属于 `SCN_013_healthcare_patient_intake_transfer`，采用患者准入核验流程。它是 `train_001` 的七月测试对应任务，并从 `train_004` 迁移病历、联系方式和风险就绪判断习惯。共享环境位于 `task_group/task_group_013/env/`，数据来自 `env/data/clinic.db`。本任务本地可见材料为 `input/payloads/target_roster.json` 和 `input/payloads/answer_template.json`。

任务定义：求解者需要处理名单 `NPI-JUL-02` 中的 `P007` 到 `P013`。服务日期为 `2026-07-09`，服务线为 `primary_care`，二者都记录在 `intake_rosters` 表中。输出应为 JSON，包含保险状态、处方权益状态、首选药房网络状态、生活方式风险、总体风险、最终登记状态、阻断原因代码和队列汇总。

场景适配与材料说明：这是一个真实的入诊办公室准入核验任务。`intake_rosters` 确定目标患者和日期；`patients` 提供人口学和联系方式完整性；`coverage` 提供主保险、保单、有效期、终止日期、网络状态、服务线和状态；`pbm` 提供处方权益和保单匹配；`patient_pharmacy` 与 `pharmacies` 用于判断第一优先级药房是否在网；`lifestyle` 提供吸烟、饮酒和运动；`clinical_history` 提供慢病负担、药物和过敏复杂度、近期住院及风险标志；`chart_artifacts` 可通过门户查看，本队列只有 `P010` 有当前生命体征记录，但不能消除高风险准入复核。

解题依据：保险必须在 `2026-07-09` 为 active、日期有效、院内网络且覆盖 `primary_care`。PBM 必须 active、approved，并且付款方和保单号与保险一致。药房只看患者第一优先级药房。生活方式评分沿用训练锚点：吸烟 Never/Former/Current = 0/1/2，饮酒 None/Occasional/Moderate/Heavy = 0/1/2/3，运动 5+ = -1、3-4 = 0、1-2 = 1、None 或缺失 = 2；小于等于 1 为 low，2-3 为 medium，大于等于 4 为 high。总体风险在生活方式高风险、近期急性就医、明确复杂用药核对，或慢病负担与用药/过敏复杂度组合较重时为 high；较轻的临床复杂度为 medium。没有可用的 primary-care 保险时优先判为 `rejected`，包括保险过期、服务线排除或主保险不在网；否则总体高风险为 `clinical_review`；仅行政阻断为 `hold`；全部通过为 `approved`。

关键证据：`P007` 的保险 active 且包含 primary care，但主保险网络为 out_of_network，并且因慢病和用药复杂度为总体高风险。`P008` 的 Cigna 保险已过期，第一优先级药房不在网，但 PBM 与保单匹配且 approved。`P009` 保险和药房通过，但 PBM inactive/rejected；总体风险为 medium，所以最终是行政暂缓。`P010` 保险通过，但 PBM pending，并且生活方式、急性就医和用药核对风险导致总体高风险，需要临床复核。`P011` 保险在 `2026-06-30` 终止，首选药房不在网，短信联系方式对应的电话缺失，并且总体高风险。`P012` 保险 pending 且不含 `primary_care`；PBM 和药房通过，但生活方式高风险。`P013` 不含 primary-care 服务线，PBM 保单不匹配，首选药房不在网，地址缺失，并且近期住院导致总体高风险。

评估依据：评估器包含八个整点评分项：SP001 保险状态，权重 2；SP002 处方权益状态，权重 2；SP003 药房状态，权重 2；SP004 生活方式风险，权重 2；SP005 总体风险，权重 2；SP006 登记状态，权重 3；SP007 阻断原因代码集合，权重 3；SP008 按登记状态和风险分类的汇总计数，权重 1。评分覆盖保险、处方权益、药房网络、生活方式风险、总体临床风险、最终登记决策、阻断原因归因和汇总聚合。患者列表按 ID 归一化，阻断原因按集合比较。每个评分项只能得满分或零分。

迁移设计：主要训练锚点是 `train_001`，它提供同一准入核验家族的经验：区分保险、PBM、首选药房、生活方式风险、总体风险、联系方式和人口学阻断、最终状态优先级以及汇总口径。`train_004` 辅助锚定“病历或联系方式存在”不等于“就绪”，以及风险信号需要单独判断。这里的 SP001 到 SP007 都依赖迁移，尤其是区分 PBM 保单不匹配和一般 PBM 无效、只使用第一优先级药房、在保险可用时把高风险转为临床复核、以及只列出失败闸口的原因码。任务自身难点来自七月服务日期、新患者、主保险不在网、六月底终止的保险、第二优先级在网药房干扰项，以及 PBM 与保险冲突。

常见错误：容易把 active 但 out-of-network 的保险当成有效；漏掉 `P011` 在七月服务日前已终止；把 `P008` 的第二优先级在网药房当成首选药房；忽略 `P013` 的 PBM 保单号不匹配；忘记运动缺失会提高生活方式风险；在保险可用但总体高风险时错误判为 `hold`；或者为已经通过的闸口添加阻断原因。
