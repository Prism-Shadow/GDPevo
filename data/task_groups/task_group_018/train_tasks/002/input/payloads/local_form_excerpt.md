# Local Form Excerpt

Document: 22nd Judicial District Extended Payment Plan
Revision: 2024-03-12
Visible form label: Extended Payment Plan Agreement

The form header asks for Defendant/Debtor and Case # / Account #. If no separate case number or account number has been opened for a traffic violation matter, the citation number is used as the account reference.

The balance section is labeled Case/Account Balance.

The clerk notes area is labeled Action Table(s) / Notes.

The payment clause is headed TERMS of PAYMENT. It records that the agreement is entered after disposition, the first due date, the monthly payment amount, and any final remainder installment when the balance does not divide evenly.

An obsolete footer in an older copy references a $25 payment-plan service charge. The 2024 revision does not make that charge automatic; use current court policy before treating any account-management amount as due.
