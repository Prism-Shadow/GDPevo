# Local Payment Form Excerpt

Document: 27th Judicial District Payment Plan Agreement
Revision: 2025-01-10
Visible form label: Payment Plan Agreement
Local policy reference: POL-OR27-EPP

The form header asks for Defendant Name, Citation #, and Account Reference. If no separate court account exists for a traffic citation, the citation number is used as the account reference.

The balance block is labeled Total Balance Due.

The schedule block is labeled Payment Schedule.

The clerk annotation area is labeled Clerk Notes.

The payment clause has fields for agreement date, first due date, monthly payment amount, installment count, and a final remainder line.

Training copies and voided drafts attached behind the form show sample account references OR27-TR-2201A, OR27-TR-2208B, OR27-TR-2219-VOID, and OR27-TR-2291.

An old vendor insert refers to a $20 payment-plan setup charge. The active 2025 local payment policy does not make that charge automatic.
