## English

Data/source lineage: This is `task_group_018` / `test_001`, based on scenario `SCN_018_court_clerk_disposition_orders_and_financial_entries` and source examples `E001`, `E002`, and `E003`. It uses the shared clerk operations environment through the public HTTP API plus the task-local payload `input/payloads/columbia_criminal_register_packet.json`. The payload is a Columbia County criminal disposition register packet for `2025-07-02`.

Task definition: The solver must produce a clerk-ready JSON register for six Columbia criminal cases. Solver-visible inputs are `prompt.txt`, `answer_template.json`, the local register packet, and the shared environment at `{ENV_BASE_URL}`. The output is sorted by `case_number` and includes disposition posture, defense type, source-precedence audit fields, scheduled-fee audit fields, restitution treatment, live-ledger before/after reconciliation, docket action/readiness fields, aggregate recomputation fields, and reduced controlled audit summaries for source precedence and financial reconciliation.

Material map: The local packet identifies the six target matters, bench-card hearing outcomes, counsel desk review log facts, local cautions, and an unapproved finance scratch workpaper. The workpaper is intentionally noisy and now includes candidate labels such as `RESTITUTION`, obsolete or unsupported fee candidates, statute labels, stale status hints, and incomplete fee-code sets. The environment supplies live case records, attorney records, docket history and corrections, Columbia criminal fee schedules, payment policy, live financial obligations, and stale export records.

Solution basis: The standard answer uses live display names, keeps prior disposition dates where no new order was entered, applies hearing outcomes only to facts actually resolved at the hearing, uses the counsel desk review log for final representation, and treats stale exports and finance scratch rows as noncontrolling. Scheduled fee components are limited to supported fee rows. Restitution is reported as separate principal in `restitution_amount`, `assessed_total`, and ledger totals, not as an approved fee code. Existing live financial orders are retained or confirmed when no new assessment is ordered; `24-COL-01002` is reissued because the hearing creates the entry-ready conviction and warrant recall; `25-COL-01001` creates a new `385.00` assessment. `24-COL-01003` is a prior disposition confirmed from the live record over a stale-export conflict, with a financial-review entry posture rather than a new conviction entry. Version `1.5` keeps only reduced `source_precedence_summary` and `financial_reconciliation_summary` fields that are inferable from row-level source, fee, restitution, and ledger conventions. It removes the brittle summary labels for noncontrolling stale-export rows, ledger-delta row lists, financial-review/deferred-update case lists, and ledger action counts.

Evaluation basis: The evaluator has ten exact-match scoring points, raw weights totaling 16. SP001 weight 1 checks metadata, case set, names, and row order. SP002 weight 2 checks case posture, disposition dates, charge-result codes, and probation months. SP003 weight 3 checks the train-anchored defense verification convention: final defense type and representation source. SP004 weight 3 checks verified-defense source on carry-forward and prior-order rows where direct solvers tended to fall back to live appearance records. SP005 weight 1 checks disposition source precedence for each row. SP006 weight 1 checks scheduled-fee-only components, excluded/added candidate fee codes, restitution policy, restitution principal, and assessed totals. SP007 weight 1 checks live-ledger before/after principal, deltas, and ledger action. SP008 weight 1 checks stable aggregate counts, total restitution, live-ledger-before total, and new-assessment count. SP009 weight 1 checks stable aggregate recomputation fields and row sets. SP010 weight 2 checks the reduced source and financial summaries for verified-defense source, controlling disposition-source rows, scheduled-fee additions, restitution-principal rows, zero-delta reissues, and new-assessment rows. The rework keeps the scoring on controlled fields and row-level conventions learned from train, gives the verified-defense carry-forward rule its intended transfer weight, and keeps finance/ledger checks as exact business checks without letting their task-specific noise erase observed train-skill gain.

Transfer design: This is a test task. The high-weight transfer-dependent scoring points are SP003, SP004, and SP006; SP005, SP007, and SP008 also depend on train-derived conventions. `train_001` teaches that draft finance imports are nonfinal, attorney verification can control representation, active criminal fee schedules control fee components, unsupported quick-pick/statute labels are excluded, restitution is principal rather than a synthetic fee component, and ledger amounts must be recomputed. `train_004` teaches that live records normally outrank stale exports, hearing notes alter only reviewed facts, fee-code sets and current ledger principal are scored business results, deltas use live ledger principal as the before value, docket action depends on whether the case is retained, adjusted, reissued, or newly assessed, and aggregates must be recomputed from row outputs. What changes here is the Columbia county schedule, six-case scale, mixed prior/new dispositions, and Columbia-specific live data discovery.

## 中文

数据来源：本任务是 `task_group_018` 的 `test_001`，来源于场景 `SCN_018_court_clerk_disposition_orders_and_financial_entries` 以及示例 `E001`、`E002`、`E003`。任务通过公开 HTTP API 使用共享 clerk operations 环境，并使用任务本地材料 `input/payloads/columbia_criminal_register_packet.json`。该材料是 Columbia County 在 `2025-07-02` 的刑事处分登记包。

任务定义：求解者需要为六件 Columbia 刑事案件生成书记员可用的 JSON 登记表。可见输入包括 `prompt.txt`、`answer_template.json`、本地登记包，以及 `{ENV_BASE_URL}` 的共享环境。输出按 `case_number` 排序，包含处分状态、辩护类型、来源优先级审计、排除和补入的费用候选、赔偿处理、实时账册 before/after 对账、案卷动作/准备状态、汇总重算字段，以及缩减后的来源优先级和财务对账 summary audit 对象。

材料地图：本地包给出六个目标案件、庭审 bench card 结果、counsel desk review log、本地 caution 和未批准的财务 scratch workpaper。该 workpaper 是有意设置的噪声，包含 `RESTITUTION` 这类候选标签、过期或不支持的费用候选、法规标签、过期状态提示和不完整费用集合。共享环境提供实时案件记录、律师记录、案卷历史和修正、Columbia 刑事费用表、付款政策、实时财务义务和过期导出记录。

答案依据：标准答案使用实时系统中的姓名；没有新命令的案件保留既有处分日期；庭审材料只改变本次庭审实际处理的事实；counsel desk review log 用于确定最终代理类型；过期导出和财务 scratch 行都不是控制性来源。费用组件只限于费用表支持的行。赔偿金额作为单独 principal 写入 `restitution_amount`、`assessed_total` 和账册总额，不作为批准费用代码。没有新评估命令的案件保留或确认实时财务命令；`24-COL-01002` 因本次庭审形成可录入定罪并撤销 warrant，需要 reissue；`25-COL-01001` 新建 `385.00` 评估。`24-COL-01003` 是实时记录优先于过期导出的既有处分确认，录入状态是财务复核，而不是新的定罪录入。版本 `1.5` 只保留可以从行级来源、费用、赔偿和账册惯例推导出的缩减版 `source_precedence_summary` 与 `financial_reconciliation_summary` 字段。它移除了过于脆弱的 summary 标签：非控制 stale-export 行、ledger-delta 行列表、financial-review/deferred-update 案件列表和 ledger action counts。

评估依据：评估器包含十个 exact-match 评分点，原始权重合计 16。SP001 权重 1，检查元数据、案件集合、姓名和顺序。SP002 权重 2，检查案件状态、处分日期、指控结果和缓刑月数。SP003 权重 3，检查训练可迁移的辩护核验惯例：最终辩护类型和代理来源。SP004 权重 3，检查 carry-forward 和 prior-order 行上的辩护核验来源，这些行是 direct 求解者容易回退到 live appearance record 的位置。SP005 权重 1，检查每行处分来源优先级。SP006 权重 1，检查 scheduled-fee-only 组件、排除/补入的候选费用、赔偿策略、赔偿 principal 和 assessed total。SP007 权重 1，检查实时账册 before/after principal、delta 和账册动作。SP008 权重 1，检查稳定的汇总计数、赔偿总额、live-ledger-before 总额和新建评估计数。SP009 权重 1，检查稳定的汇总重算字段和行集合。SP010 权重 2，检查缩减后的来源和财务 summary，包括辩护核验来源、控制性处分来源、补入 scheduled fee、赔偿 principal 行、零 delta reissue 行和新建评估行。本次重构保留受控字段 exact match，把评分重点放在训练中可学到的行级惯例上，提高辩护核验 carry-forward 规则的迁移权重，并让财务/ledger 点继续作为低权重精确业务检查。

迁移设计：这是测试任务。高权重且依赖训练迁移的评分点是 SP003、SP004 和 SP006；SP005、SP007 和 SP008 也依赖训练中学到的惯例。`train_001` 可以迁移的经验包括：draft finance import 不是最终依据，律师核验 memo 可以控制代理类型，生效中的刑事费用表控制费用组件，不支持的 quick-pick 或法规标签要排除，赔偿是 principal 而不是合成费用组件，以及需要重算账册金额。`train_004` 可以迁移的经验包括：实时记录通常优先于过期导出，庭审 note 只改变实际复核的事实，费用代码集合和实时 ledger principal 是关键业务结果，delta 以实时 ledger principal 为 before 值，docket action 取决于保留、调整、重发或新建评估，汇总必须从行级输出重算。本测试的变化是 Columbia 县费用表、六案规模、既有与新处分混合，以及 Columbia 特定实时数据探索。
