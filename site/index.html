Results · two harnesses, same shape结果 · 两套 harness，同一种形状

Skills make agents more accurate while spending fewer tokens. Skill 让 agent 更准,同时花更少的 token。

Each group has 5 train and 5 held-out test tasks. We compare a cold-start baseline (no_skill) against two skill-writers — demonstration (learn from worked examples) and reflection (learn from its own mistakes) — and measure the lift on tasks the skill-writer never saw. 每组有 5 个 train、5 个 held-out test。我们用冷启动基线（no_skill）对比两种 skill-writer —— demonstration（从样例中学）和 reflection（从自己的错误中学）—— 衡量在 skill-writer 没见过的任务上的提升。
